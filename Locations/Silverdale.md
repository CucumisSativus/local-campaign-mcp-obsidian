A bustling market town nestled in the foothills of the Ironpeak Mountains.

## Description

Silverdale is a prosperous trading hub known for its silver mines and skilled craftsmen. The town sits at the crossroads of major trade routes, making it a melting pot of cultures and commerce.

## Population

Approximately 3,500 residents
- 60% Humans
- 20% Dwarves
- 15% Halflings
- 5% Other races

## Notable Locations

### The Silver Hammer Inn
A popular establishment run by Greta Ironforge, a retired adventurer. Known for its hearty meals and comfortable beds.

### Temple of the Morning Light
A grand temple dedicated to Pelor, led by High Priestess Elara Brightwood.

### The Guildhall
Center of trade and commerce, where merchants and craftsmen conduct their business.

## Important NPCs

- **Mayor Aldric Thornton**: The town's mayor, a shrewd politician with connections throughout the region
- **Captain Helena Stormwind**: Commander of the town guard, stern but fair
- **Master Theron Silversmith**: The most respected silversmith in town, elderly dwarf with 80 years of experience

## Rumors and Hooks

- Strange noises have been heard from the old abandoned mine to the east
- A mysterious merchant has been buying up property in the merchant district
- Bandits have been spotted on the northern trade road
