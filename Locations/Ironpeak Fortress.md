A mighty dwarven stronghold carved into the mountains north of Silverdale.

## Description

Ironpeak Fortress is an ancient dwarven citadel that has stood for over 800 years. Built deep within the Ironpeak Mountains, it serves as both a military stronghold and a thriving underground city.

## History

Founded by Clan Ironforge during the War of the Stone, the fortress has never fallen to enemy attack. Its intricate network of tunnels and halls spans multiple levels, reaching deep into the mountain's heart.

## Population

Approximately 5,000 dwarves, with a small number of human and gnome residents.

## Structure

### Upper Halls
- Main gates and trading district
- Barracks and training grounds
- Diplomatic quarters for surface visitors

### Middle Halls
- Residential districts
- The Great Forge (legendary smithing facility)
- Temple of Moradin

### Deep Halls
- Ancient vaults and treasuries
- The King's Hall (throne room)
- Sealed sections (forbidden to all but the king's guard)

## Leadership

**King Thorin Ironforge III**: Current ruler, known for his wisdom and skill in diplomacy
**General Barik Stonefist**: Commander of the fortress guard
**High Priest Durin Flameheart**: Keeper of dwarven traditions and leader of the temple

## Trade

The fortress is renowned for:
- Exceptional weapons and armor
- Rare metals and gemstones
- Dwarven ale and spirits

## Current Events

- Rumors of strange sounds coming from the sealed lower levels
- Increased orc activity in the mountain passes
- Discovery of a new vein of mithril has attracted unwanted attention

## Relations

- Strong alliance with Silverdale
- Trade agreements with several surface kingdoms
- Ongoing skirmishes with orc tribes in the mountains
