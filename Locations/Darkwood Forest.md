An ancient and mysterious forest that borders the eastern edge of Silverdale.

## Description

The Darkwood Forest is a dense, primordial woodland that has stood for thousands of years. Locals avoid venturing too deep, as strange creatures and fey beings are said to dwell within its shadowy depths.

## Features

- **Ancient Trees**: Some trees are over 500 years old, with trunks wide enough to hollow out for shelter
- **Perpetual Twilight**: Even during the day, the dense canopy creates a dim, twilight atmosphere
- **Fey Crossing**: Areas where the barrier between the Material Plane and the Feywild is thin

## Wildlife

- Dire wolves
- Giant spiders
- Owlbears
- Various fey creatures

## Notable Locations

### The Druid Circle
A sacred grove maintained by a circle of druids led by Thornbark the Ancient, an old treant.

### The Whispering Glade
A clearing where it's said you can hear voices of the forest spirits at midnight.

### The Abandoned Watchtower
An old stone tower, now covered in vines, that once served as a border outpost.

## Dangers

- Easy to get lost without a guide
- Aggressive wildlife
- Unpredictable fey encounters
- Areas of wild magic

## Quest Hooks

- The druids need help investigating a corruption spreading through the forest
- A noble's son went missing after entering the forest three days ago
- Ancient ruins deep in the forest are said to contain powerful artifacts
